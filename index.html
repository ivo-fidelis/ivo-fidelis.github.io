Data Visualization for User Growth Insights


The Problem

The company is looking to understand and improve User Growth in the target companies.

Tech Challenge

  • Manipulate raw data and analyze conversion.

Business Challenge

  • Evaluate clients' current performance in user growth and provide key insights toward overall growth in user acquisition.

The Solution

  1. Understanding metrics and manipulating data to perform funnel analysis.
  2. Exploratory data analysis with Google Data Studio.
  3. Creation of graphs that showed the performance of users' growth and indicated a path to be followed.

Results and Insights

The analysis showed that the performance of user growth varied greatly from company to company. Fortunately, the data indicated two paths to be followed to improve growth.

The Activation Strategy

When the conversion rate from Sign Up to Purchased is high (or higher than the sign-up rate), we should focus on bringing more people to sign up. In some cases, they'll most likely become a paid user.

The Conversion Strategy

When the sign-up rate is high and the number of purchases in that partner is low (very few final conversions), we should focus on getting more signed users to purchase a plan.

In order to improve user growth for these companies, we need to have more targeted communication and promotion strategies focused either on:

  1. Activating the non-signed users;
  2. Converting the non-paying signed users.
